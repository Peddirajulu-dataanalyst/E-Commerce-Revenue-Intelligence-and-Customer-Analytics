📌 Overview 

This project analyzes e-commerce sales data to uncover insights related to revenue performance, customer behavior, profitability, and operational efficiency. 
The goal is to support data-driven decision-making through SQL analysis and interactive dashboards. 

🎯 Objectives 

. Analyze sales and profit trends 
. Identify top-performing customers (Pareto 80/20) 
. Perform customer segmentation (RFM) 
. Evaluate customer retention and churn 
. Measure impact of discounts on profitability 
. Analyze returns and their impact on revenue 

🛠️ Tools & Technologies 

. SQL (MySQL) 
. Python (Pandas, NumPy) 
. Tableau 
. Power BI

📂 Dataset 

The dataset consists of: 

. orders.csv → transactional sales data 
. customers.csv → customer information 
. products.csv → product details 
. returns.csv → returned orders 

 🔍 Key Analysis Performed 

1. Sales Performance 

. Revenue and profit trends 
. Category and region analysis 
. Channel performance (Online vs Retail) 

2. Top 20% Customers (Pareto Analysis) 

. Top 20% customers contribute ~68% of total revenue 
. Highlights revenue concentration and dependency on key customers 

3. Customer Retention Analysis 

. Repeat vs One-time customers 
. Identifies customer loyalty and churn risk 

4. RFM Segmentation 

. Customers segmented into: 

VIP 

. Loyal 
. Active 
. Inactive 
. At Risk 
. Churned 

5. Discount vs Profit Analysis 

. Higher discounts reduce profit margins 
. Excessive discounting negatively impacts profitability 

6. Return Impact Analysis 

. Return rate and revenue loss measured 
. Key return reasons identified (damaged, wrong item, delivery issues) 

📊 Dashboards 

Power BI Dashboard 

. KPI cards (Revenue, Profit, Return Rate, Lost Revenue) 
. Customer segmentation 
. Sales performance insigh 

Tableau Dashboard 

. Interactive visualizations 
. Customer behavior analysis 
. Return and discount impact insights 

📈 Key Insights 

. Revenue is highly concentrated among top customers 
. Customer retention is strong but one-time buyers pose risk 
. High discounts significantly reduce profitability 
. Returns contribute to revenue and profit loss 
. Operational inefficiencies impact business performance

🚀 Business Recommendations 

. Focus on retaining high-value (VIP) customers 
. Optimize discount strategies to protect margins 
. Improve product quality and logistics to reduce returns 
. Target at-risk customers with re-engagement strategies 

💡 Business Impact 

. Improved profitability through optimized pricing 
. Increased customer lifetime value 
. Reduced losses from returns 
. Better strategic decision-making 

📌 Conclusion 

This project demonstrates how data analytics can uncover critical business insights, enabling organizations to improve revenue, customer retention, and operational efficiency.
